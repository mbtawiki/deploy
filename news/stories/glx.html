Green Line Extension (GLX)

The Green Line currently terminates at Lechmere Station, but with the up and coming Green Line Extension (or GLX for short), the Green Line will soon terminate at Union Square and College Avenue. Green Line E cars will run from Heath St. to Union Square, meanwhile D cars will run from Riverside to College Ave. After the relocated Lechmere Station will be Union Square (on the Union Square branch) and Washington Street, Gilman Square, Lowell Street, Ball Square, and College Avenue on the Medford Branch. The Green Line Extension is planned to be opened in December of 2021, and will run for 4.3 additional miles past Lechmere.
